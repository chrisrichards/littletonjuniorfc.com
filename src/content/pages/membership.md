-   ### Our Subs
    
    Club fees for 2025/26 are £200 for full members who play league matches, and £180 for those who only train. This pays for Saints Foundation coaches, pitch hire, equipment, kit, and other costs.
    
-   ### Joining us
    
    If you are looking to join the club, please contact the relevant Age Group Coordinator listed on the _Contact us_ page.
    
-   ### Paying Subs
    
    To pay your annual subs, please log into our Pitchero membership system.
    
    [Read more](https://www.pitchero.com/clubs/littletonjuniorfc/payments)
    
-   ### Your details
    
    To make any changes to your account, please log into our Pitchero membership system.
