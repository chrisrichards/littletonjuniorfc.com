-   ## Origins
    
    Littleton Junior Football Club was formed in 1974 by Les Elms for the Cubs of the 1st Winchester Scout Group. Today we provide coaching and competitive football for different age groups from under 6s to under 18s.
    
-   ![](/images/home/origins.jpg)
-   ![](/images/home/ethos.jpg)
-   ## Our Ethos
    
    We believe that fun should always come first. If you’re enjoying football, then you’re going to be better at it. We are a club for children of all ages and abilities, where football is for everyone, not just the elite few. Download our ethos document to read more.
    
    [Read more](/images/downloads/Club_Ethos_2021_pb3.pdf)
    
-   ## Coaching
    
    We are run by FA qualified parent coaches and managers. But we are supplemented by weekly or fortnightly coaches from the Saints Foundation.
    
-   ![](/images/home/coaching.jpg)
-   ![](/images/home/location.jpg)
-   ## Location
    
    Our home is at Littleton Rec, just outside Winchester, UK. But the high demand for places in the club means we train at a range of different venues around the city.
